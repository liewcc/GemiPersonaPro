# 🔖 Gems Bookmark & Character Consistency

Gemini "Gems" are custom versions of Gemini that you can create for specific tasks. In GemiPersonaPro, the **Gems Bookmark** feature is a powerful tool for maintaining consistency in your AI generations.

---

## 🚀 The Power of Gems: Character Consistency
One of the biggest challenges in AI generation is keeping a character's appearance the same across different scenes. Gems solve this perfectly:

1.  **The "Avatar" Strategy**: Create a Gem in your browser and upload a reference photo of your character (e.g., a "half-body" or "headshot" portrait).
2.  **Fixed Identity**: Because the Gem "remembers" the reference photo, you don't need to re-upload it every time.
3.  **Varying the Scene**: Simply send different background or action prompts to that specific Gem URL. The character stays the same, while the world around them changes!

---

## 🛠️ Managing Your Bookmarks
Navigate to the **Gems Bookmark** page from the sidebar to organize your workflows:

### Adding & Editing
- **Manual Add**: Enter a Name, the Gem's URL, and a short description.
- **🔍 Auto-Fetch**: Enter the URL and click "Auto-Fetch". The engine will automatically navigate to the Gem, scrape its official name and description, and fill out the form for you!
- **Save**: Click Save to add it to your permanent collection (`Gems_bookmark.json`).

### Quick Actions
Each saved bookmark has three main controls:
- **Apply**: ⚡ **The most important button**. Clicking this sends the Gem's URL directly to your **Gemini Setup** configuration.
- **Edit**: Modify the name or description.
- **Delete**: Remove the bookmark from your lab.

---

## 🔗 Using a Bookmarked Gem
1.  Go to the **Gems Bookmark** page.
2.  Find your desired Gem and click **Apply**.
3.  Navigate to **Gemini Setup**. You will see the `Browser URL` has been updated to your Gem's address.
4.  Enter your prompt and click **Send to Browser** or start the **Looping Process**.

---
*Tip: Use multiple Gems to switch between different "AI actors" for your projects!*
